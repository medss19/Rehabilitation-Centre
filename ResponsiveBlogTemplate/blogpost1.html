Benefits of Treatment in India for International Patients

Rajnandini Rathod
September 19, 2023

Some Benefits of Getting Mental Health & Addiction Treatment in India A remarkable shift has occurred in the mental health and addiction treatment world in recent years. More and more individuals from around the globe are turning their attention towards India as a destination for healing and recovery. If you need help with your mental health or addiction, you might be curious about addiction treatment in India.

. World-Class Treatment Facilities

When it comes to seeking mental health and addiction treatment in India, the country boasts an impressive array of world-class treatment facilities. These centres have gained recognition not only for their serene environments but also for their innovative and effective therapeutic approaches. Here are some of the prominent treatment centres in India, committed to providing top-tier care to those in need. Alpha Healing Center, Gujrat: This is an ISO-certified facility, renowned as one of India’s premier rehab centres. Alpha Healing Center offers personalised treatment plans catering to a diverse spectrum of addiction-related challenges. The Center is fortified by a seasoned team of professionals, including Psychiatrists, Clinical Psychologists, Occupational Therapists, Hypnotherapists, Art and Music Therapists, Yoga Instructors, and counsellors, all contributing to its esteemed reputation. Lotus Wellness Center, Coimbatore: Lotus represents a contemporary residential treatment centre and mental health institution, setting itself apart from conventional rehab facilities. It has been designed as a sanctuary, providing individuals with a haven for recovery, serenity, reprieve, and contentment. Lotus is steadfast in its dedication to delivering treatment and care of the highest calibre, mirroring the standards of care that one would desire for their own beloved family members. Abhasa Rehabilitation and Wellness Home, Maharashtra: At ABHASA, you’ll encounter a world of holistic therapies that harmonise the mind, body, and soul. Their offerings span from evidence-based practices to elements inspired by Eastern traditions and the natural world, creating a holistic synergy that enhances your path to well-being. Situated amidst the serenity of nature, their tranquil setting fosters an atmosphere of tranquillity and transformation. In addition to their esteemed reputations, these treatment centres leverage cutting-edge therapeutic technologies and holistic approaches to ensure the highest standard of care. Here are some innovative methods you can expect to encounter during your treatment journey in India: Biofeedback and Neurofeedback: Advanced biofeedback and neurofeedback techniques are employed to help patients gain better control over their physiological and neurological responses. These tools assist in managing conditions like anxiety, depression, and addiction cravings. Art and Expressive Therapies: Many treatment centers incorporate art, music, and expressive therapies as complementary tools for self-expression and emotional healing. These creative therapies can be especially beneficial for individuals who struggle to verbalise their emotions. Nature Therapy: Spending time in nature, known as ecotherapy or nature therapy, can have therapeutic benefits. It helps individuals reconnect with the natural world and reduce stress. As part of your daily routine, you might go on nature walks. Yoga and Meditation: Yoga and meditation techniques are widely used to promote relaxation, reduce stress, and increase mindfulness. They help individuals in recovery manage cravings and improve overall well-being.

2. Highly-Skilled Teams Indian mental health and addiction specialists are renowned for their qualifications and extensive experience. Many have received their education and training from prestigious institutions both within India and abroad. Whether it’s psychiatrists, clinical psychologists, or addiction counsellors, their expertise extends to various treatment modalities, ensuring that individuals receive well-rounded and evidence-based care that meets international standards. Recovery is rarely a one-person journey and Indian treatment centers understand the value of collaboration. At the heart of many successful programs is a multidisciplinary team consisting of psychiatrists, therapists, counsellors, and support staff. This collaborative approach ensures that each aspect of an individual’s mental health and addiction is addressed comprehensively. Here are some testimonials from international clients who underwent treatment in centres located in India: “I have lived all my life as a wanderer, not a substance I had not tried. Checked myself into rehabs from all around the world all the way from US to Thailand. Felt empty from within before I chose India, I came to Sanctum as a broken man, just wanted a break from life and today, exactly 170 days after I left, I feel like this was my life’s calling all along. I reconciled with my ex-wife, been clean and happy. And with very passing day I thank God for his beautiful sanctuary located in the spiritual land of India called Sanctum.” – David Cooper who underwent treatment in Sanctum, Delhi. “Coming Here helped me realize a lot about myself. I was extremely dependent on Cannabis and I found my light here. Not only was my addiction taken into account, but also other things like my anxiety and depression, and I have found better ways of dealing with it” – Anonymous client from UK who underwent treatment in Samarpan, Maharashtra.
